About Us

J Innovative Engineering
Turns – JIET

Incorporated in the year 2019, JIET – J Innovative Engineering Turns is an ISO 9001:2015 Certified Company delivering process-oriented engineering solutions worldwide.

We offer you solutions that address a wide range of requirements for customers in diverse industries — from individual disciplines such as Electrical, Mechanical, Automation, and Utility Engineering, to comprehensive workflows from the initial product idea to development, design, and production.

"We are a total solution provider — from Electrical, Instrumentation and Automation to Pharmaceutical Equipment, SS Ducting, Pipelines and Plant Modifications."

Our products are manufactured in accordance with updated quality parameters. Owing to these quality testing parameters, we attain excellence and leave no stone unturned in performance. The advanced technical knowledge keeps us ahead in the competitive market.
